Elegant and Dynamic Design

  • Sporty and Striking Look: Features a sporty rear spoiler, dual exhaust outlets, and an electric sunroof for a bold and performance-inspired appearance.
  • High-Tech LED Headlamps: Equipped with advanced LED headlights, the EMPOW delivers superior visibility and a modern, assertive presence on the road.
  • 18” Aluminum Wheels with Michelin Tires: A perfect blend of modern style, lightweight durability, and high-performance grip for an unmatched driving experience.
  • Sleek LED Taillights: Eye-catching and contemporary taillights leave a bold impression while ensuring excellent rear visibility for a safer, more enjoyable drive.
  • Power Adjustable & Folding Side Mirrors: Enhancing comfort and visibility, these mirrors are seamlessly integrated into the vehicle’s sleek overall design.

Limitless Acceleration: 0–100 km/h in Just 5.7 Seconds with 265 HP

​Experience the thrill of the high-performance EMPOW 2.0T Sport engine combined with cutting-edge driver assistance systems.​

  • Smart Adaptive Cruise Control with Radar (ACC) & Integrated Cruise Assist (ICA): Automatically adjusts your speed to maintain a safe distance from the vehicle ahead, delivering a smoother, semi-autonomous driving experience.​
  • Intelligent Speed Limit Recognition System: Reads road speed limits and helps ensure driving compliance.​
  • Traffic Jam Assist (TJA): Eases stop-and-go driving by helping maintain safe spacing and lane positioning in congested traffic.
  • ​Self-Learning Transmission Function: Adapts to your personal driving style over time for smoother gear shifts and a more intuitive driving feel.​
  • Neutral Control Function: Disengages the gear (D) during idle such as at traffic lights to improve fuel efficiency and vehicle performance.
  • ​Manual Mode & Paddle Shifters: Simulates manual transmission performance, enhancing gearshift response and strengthening the connection between driver and machine

  • Bosch ESP10.0 Electronic Stability Program: Ensures confident control in all driving conditions by maintaining vehicle stability during turns or emergency maneuvers.

  • DP-EPS Electric Power Steering: Delivers enhanced torque transfer and more direct steering feedback for greater driving precision and responsiveness.

Innovative Aesthetics

  • Elegant Yet Sporty Interior: The interior of the Empow strikes the perfect balance between elegance and sportiness, with premium materials, stylish accents, and comfortable, supportive seats that enhance the driving experience.
  • 7-inch instrument panel: With a color LCD multi-information display, the panel provides a clear and customizable view of essential driving data, offering convenience, clarity, and ease of use while on the road.
  • 10.25-in. Color LCD Screen Display: The Empow's large 10.25-inch color LCD screen display delivers stunning graphics and intuitive controls, providing an immersive and engaging user experience while offering easy access to entertainment, navigation, and vehicle settings.
  • 6 Sound Speakers: These speakers deliver crisp and immersive sound quality, providing an exceptional audio experience that elevates every drive.
  • First & Second Row USB Ports: Provide easy access to device charging and connectivity, ensuring that you and your passengers are always connected and powered up on the go.
  • Smartphone Connection (Carplay & FunDrive Link): Seamlessly integrates your smartphone with the car's infotainment system, allowing you to access your favorite apps, music, and contacts while keeping your focus on the road ahead.
  • Wireless Charger: Provides a hassle-free and convenient charging solution, allowing you to keep your smartphone powered up and within reach without the clutter of cables and cords..
  • Adaptive Cruise Control: This technology automatically adjusts your driving speed to maintain a safe distance from the vehicle in front of you, providing a stress-free and comfortable driving experience.
  • Autonomous Emergency Braking: designed to automatically apply the brakes to prevent or minimize a collision with another vehicle, providing an extra layer of safety and peace of mind on the road.

Experience the Thrill of the High-Performance EMPOW Sport Engine

  • Next-Generation 2.0L GDI Turbo Engine: Delivers high power, a top speed of up to 250 km/h, fuel efficiency, and trusted reliability.

  • 8-Speed Aisin Automatic Transmission
  • 0–100 km/h Acceleration in 5.70 Seconds

  • Four Driving Modes: Track, Sport, Comfort, and Eco

  • Idle Stop & Go (ISG): Engine auto shut-off at full stop for enhanced fuel efficiency.

  • Electronic Parking Brake (EPB) with Auto Hold

  • ​Comfort-Tuned Suspension System: Front: Independent MacPherson Suspension. Rear: Independent Multi-Link Beam Suspension.

  • ​Advanced Airbag System: Dual front airbags, front side airbags, and side curtain airbags

Agile and Dynamic Handling for Maximum Control on the Road

  • Electronic Stability Program (ESP): built to automatically assist in stabilizing your vehicle when it notices a brief loss of control.
  • Hydraulic Brake Assistant (HBA): Uses the high-pressure fluid from the power steering pump to "boost" the pressure and sensation of your brake pedal.​
  • Hill-start Hold Control (HHC): a driver assistance technology that stops the vehicle from sliding backwards when you're driving it from a stop on an uphill or slope.
  • Electric Brakeforce distribution (EBD): System that minimizes stopping distance and stabilizes braking performance in vehicles with heavy loads on the rear wheels.
