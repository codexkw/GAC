Performance

Seamless Control, Dynamic Force

Unleash the Power

Experience the thrill of the EMPOW's high-performance engine.

  • New Generation 1.5L Turbo GDI Engine: High power, low fuel consumption and reliable quality.
  • 7-speed Wet Dual-Clutch Transmission.
  • Electronic Parking Brake (EPB) with Autohold.
  • Suspension Built for Comfort: Front McPherson independent; rear multi-link beam independent.
  • Airbags: Dual front, front side and side-curtain airbags.

Precision Handling

Agile and dynamic handling for maximum control on the road.

  • Electronic Stability Program (ESP): Automatically helps stabilise the vehicle at the first sign of lost control.
  • Hydraulic Brake Assistant (HBA): Boosts brake-pedal pressure and feel for stronger stopping power.
  • Hill-start Hold Control (HHC): Stops the vehicle rolling backwards when setting off uphill.
  • Electronic Brakeforce Distribution (EBD): Minimises stopping distance and stabilises braking under load.

Engineering Marvel

Innovative technologies and superior craftsmanship for unmatched performance and efficiency.

  • Electric Power Steering (EPS): A key technology for highly automated driving.
  • Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS): Real-time tyre-pressure information to the driver.
  • Anti-lock Braking System (ABS): Prevents wheel lock under heavy braking so you keep steering.
  • Max. Torque: 270 N·m.
