Safety

AION ES is equipped with a range of safety technologies that ensure peace of mind on every journey

Electronic Stability Program (ESP)

Automatically helps stabilise the vehicle the moment it detects a loss of grip or control.

Anti-lock Braking System (ABS)

Prevents the wheels from locking under hard braking so you keep full steering control.

Hill Hold Control (HHC)

Holds the vehicle in place on an incline, preventing roll-back when setting off uphill.
