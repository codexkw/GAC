GAC Motor in Kuwait is distributed by Mutawa Alkadi Automotive — pairing world-class vehicles with a trusted nationwide network.

Our Story

Engineering that moves Kuwait

GAC Motor designs and builds a modern range of SUVs, sedans and electric vehicles recognised for quality, refinement and value. Across Kuwait, Mutawa Alkadi Automotive brings that range to drivers with showrooms, certified service centres and genuine parts.

From the family-ready GS8 and M8 to the high-performance EMPOW R and the all-electric AION line-up, every GAC is built to deliver confidence on every journey.
