Privacy Policy

How Mutawa Alkadi Automotive collects, uses and protects the personal information you share with us.

Last updated: June 2026

Mutawa Alkadi Automotive Company (“Mutawa Alkadi”, “we”, “us” or “our”), the authorised distributor of GAC Motor in the State of Kuwait, respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices you have. It applies to this website and to the forms, enquiries and services we provide.

1. Information we collect

What we collect

When you contact us, request a quote, book a test drive or service, enquire about a recall, or otherwise interact with us, we may collect:

  • Contact details — your name, title, email address and phone number.
  • Enquiry details — the model, branch, message or service you are interested in.
  • Vehicle details — where relevant, your vehicle identification number (VIN) or registration for service and recall enquiries.
  • Technical data — basic information your browser sends automatically, such as device type and pages visited, used to operate and improve the website.
2. How we use your information

Purposes

We use your information to respond to your enquiries, arrange test drives, quotations, sales and aftersales service, manage warranty and safety/recall campaigns, maintain our customer relationship, meet legal and regulatory obligations, and — only with your consent — to send you marketing information about GAC products and offers.

3. Sharing your information

Who we share it with

We may share your information with GAC Motor and its affiliates, with service providers who act on our behalf, and with authorities or other parties where required by law for compliance, safety campaigns, government enquiries or similar legal process. We do not sell your personal information. Any third parties who handle your data on our behalf are required to protect it and use it only for the purposes we specify.

4. Data retention

How long we keep it

We keep your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy, including to satisfy any legal, accounting, warranty or reporting requirements, after which it is securely deleted or anonymised.

5. Cookies

Cookies & similar technologies

This website may use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences and understand how the site is used so we can improve it. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings; disabling them may affect some features of the site.

6. Data security

Keeping your data safe

We use appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against loss, misuse and unauthorised access. While no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, we work to safeguard your data and limit access to those who need it.

7. Your rights

Your choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and you may withdraw your consent to marketing at any time. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

8. Children’s privacy

Children

This website and our services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

9. Changes to this policy

Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

10. Contact us

Get in touch

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal information, please call us on 1833334 or visit our Contact page.
