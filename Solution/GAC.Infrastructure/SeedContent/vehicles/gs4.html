A Design That Reflects Distinction

An elegant exterior that blends sophistication with dynamic character, the GS4 MAX embodies a modern identity that reflects confidence and individuality. Featuring 20-inch aluminum wheels, it delivers a sporty, refined look with stable performance in all conditions, while sleek, flowing lines express strength and assurance.

Intelligent LED headlights shine with clarity and style, offering excellent visibility alongside a striking visual presence. The daytime running lights add a contemporary touch that enhances the vehicle's distinctive identity.

The panoramic glass sunroof creates an open, luxurious feel, and with hidden door handles and power-folding side mirrors, every detail of the GS4 MAX is designed to seamlessly combine elegance with practicality at its finest.

Interior Design — Luxury in Every Detail

A refined cabin that brings together comfortable leather seats, intelligent multi-color ambient lighting, and a panoramic glass roof with an electric sunshade—creating journeys filled with luxury and comfort. Seamless convenience comes standard with electrically hidden door handles, a power tailgate, and a 6-way electrically adjustable driver's seat.

  • Perforated leather seats with Zero-Pressure technology: Unmatched comfort for long journeys.
  • Front seat ventilation: Keeps you cool and refreshed in all climates.
  • 6-way power-adjustable driver's seat: Easily find your ideal driving position.
  • Multi-color ambient lighting: Adds a refined touch and a relaxing atmosphere tailored to your mood.
  • Power tailgate: Effortless opening and closing for a premium, practical experience.
  • Anti-pinch power windows: Safety and comfort at the touch of a button.

Smart Technologies at Your Fingertips

Advanced technology keeps you connected while delivering a modern driving experience.

  • An intelligent driving experience with a 10.1-inch high-resolution touchscreen and a 7-inch digital instrument cluster.
  • Stay connected at all times with Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, Bluetooth, and remote vehicle control via the smartphone app.

Performance

Powerful performance

Performance That Inspires

The new GS4 MAX delivers true power with its 2.0L Turbo GDI engine, generating 248 hp and 400 Nm of torque for a smooth yet exhilarating driving experience.

The 8-speed AISIN automatic transmission ensures precise shifts and exceptional driving smoothness, while the AWD system provides optimal stability and control on all types of roads.

Choose from 7 driving modes for a tailored experience on every journey—whether navigating city streets, cruising on the highway, or exploring light off-road adventures.

Safety First

The GS4 MAX puts your safety at the forefront, featuring 8 airbags, high-definition surround-view cameras, and intelligent driver assistance systems.

  • Advanced safety technologies—such as Adaptive Cruise Control, Forward Collision Warning, Automatic Emergency Braking, and Lane Keeping Assist—offer a Level 2 semi-autonomous driving experience.
  • Complete peace of mind on the road.
  • Additional systems, including Traffic Jam Assist, Speed Limit Recognition, Electronic Stability Control, and Tire Pressure Monitoring, ensure a safe and stable journey in all conditions.

Advanced Driving — Intelligence That Makes a Difference

Safe and smooth driving supported by intelligent systems that anticipate the road ahead.

  • Intelligent Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC): Maintains a safe distance from vehicles ahead.
  • Integrated Cruise Assist (ICA): Adjusts speed according to road conditions for a smoother experience.
  • Forward Collision Warning (FCW): Alerts you to potential collisions.
  • Lane Keeping Assist (LKA) & Lane Departure Warning (LDW): Helps you stay in your lane at all times.
  • Intelligent Speed Recognition: Reads traffic signs and adjusts speed automatically.
  • Traffic Jam Assist (TJA): Eases driving in congestion and reduces stress.
  • High Beam Assist (HMA): Adjusts headlights for optimal visibility without disturbing other drivers.
